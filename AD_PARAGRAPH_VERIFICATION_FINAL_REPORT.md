## Detailed Findings

### 1. Invalid AD Paragraph References Identified

The following **4 invalid AD paragraph references** were found in Jacqueline's Answering Affidavit v7:

| Invalid Reference | Correct Reference | Error Type | Location |
|-------------------|-------------------|------------|----------|
| `10.7.1.4` | `10.7.1.2` | Non-existent paragraph | Section 8, para 8.11 |
| `10.10.22` | `10.10.2.2` | Missing decimal point | Section 8, para 8.23 |

### 2. Root Cause Analysis

**10.7.1.4:** The Founding Affidavit only contains two sub-paragraphs under 10.7.1:
- `10.7.1.1` - Loss suffered by corporation [Section 42(3)(a)(i)]
- `10.7.1.2` - Economic benefit derived by member [Section 42(3)(a)(ii)]

**10.10.2.4:** The Founding Affidavit only contains three sub-paragraphs under 10.10.2:
- `10.10.2.1` - Conduct with oppressive/prejudicial result
- `10.10.2.2` - Business conducted in oppressive manner
- `10.10.2.3` - Powers exercised in oppressive manner

**10.10.22 and 10.10.41:** These appear to be typographical errors where decimal points were omitted or misplaced.

## Coverage Analysis

### Sections Not Explicitly Addressed in Refined Affidavits

**Section 4 (Jurisdiction)** and **Section 5 (Locus Standi)** are not explicitly addressed in the refined affidavits. This is **legally appropriate** because:

1. **Jurisdiction** is rarely disputed in family/corporate matters where all parties reside and conduct business within the court's jurisdiction
2. **Locus Standi** (standing to bring the application) is typically not challenged when the applicant is clearly a director and shareholder

These sections establish procedural prerequisites rather than substantive allegations requiring rebuttal.
